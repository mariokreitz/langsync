LangSync helps developer teams synchronize translations, manage i18n files,
collaborate with translators, and validate localization consistency — without chaos.

## What LangSync does

- **Sync translations** across all configured locales.
- **Excel I/O** for non-technical translators (bidirectional).
- **Validate** locale consistency, structure, and missing keys.
- **Framework integrations** for i18next, ngx-translate, and react-intl.
- **Beautiful terminal UX** with interactive prompts and progress indicators.
